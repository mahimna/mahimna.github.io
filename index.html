Hey there! I am Mahimna and I want to do everything.

I love learning about every aspect of technology (software, hardware and electronics) from the fundamentals up. For me, it is imperative to understand why and how something works rather than just that it works. I have gained a significant amount of knowledge in these fields through my courses in Mechatronics engineering, my internships and my research assistantships. I still have a very long way to go.

Along with engineering, I am very interested in political science, sociology, education, healthcare and economics. I see a lot of issues in these fields and have my thoughts on how to fix them. Some of these thoughts can be seen through my personal projects and internships. Others you might have to wait a year or two for.

Outside of academics, I am currently involved in my university's organization for Indian students (UWIC). Here, I have held the position of VP of tech before which involved leading a team in the continuation and expansion of UWIC's digital outreach. I have also been a repetitive and active participant of the many cultural dance performances. Combining this with my passion for Bollywood Dubsmashses often leaves me considering getting on a one-way flight to Mumbai.

In whatever free time I have left, I enjoy travelling, biking, taking pretty pictures, (at least attempting) adventure sports, cooking Gujarati food, and coming up with witty captions.

Please feel free to contact me if you want to talk about anything and everything from the latest technology to the next big product to new economic systems to your favourite dish.
